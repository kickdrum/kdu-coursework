Team Outings It is not all about work at Kickdrum. We are always looking for an excuse to break away to an outdoor team event or lunch. We try to do one or two overnight trips with the team every six months. We do monthly lunches picking a different hangout or watering-hole in and around Koramangala each month. Our team also participates in “community giving” and “marathon” type events from time to time. These outings are a fantastic way to bond with colleagues who aren’t working on the same project or product.

Collaborative Workspace We have a hip and human-centric office that allows our engineers to perform at the top of their game. The setting is casual and functional and team members work at their open desks, cafe-style tables, and soft couches. Pick a nook or pop into a meeting room to tackle tasks or project that need focus. We have a break area that is well-stocked with quick bites and healthy picks to satisfy our engineers’ hunger pangs. We have table-tennis, a pool table, carrom and several board games to unwind during the workday.
